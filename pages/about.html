    Welcome to Rail Haus. I’m Donny and we are so excited to be in downtown Dover. We're thrilled to present an extraordinary gathering place for beer enthusiasts, families, and four-legged friends alike. With a strong commitment to community, diversity, and inclusivity, we're proud to have both veteran and minority owners at the helm.

    Our mission is simple: A Great Beer Garden, all the time, for everyone.

    But our goal is much harder. We aim to change the long-held views of downtown Dover through positive development, strong community ties, and improving quality of life for our team, our neighbors, our customers. A vibrant downtown is tough to measure but you know when you see it. That’s our target. - Donny
